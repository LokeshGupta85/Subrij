Why Subrij

Our Values

➤ Integrity

  • Integrity is our most important value. Putting our clients’ best interests first is the cornerstone of our working relationships. We:
    1. provide objective, independent and unbiased advice.
    2. do not have sales quotas and are not obligated to sell specific products. ☻

➤ Competence
  • Competence of our dedicated professionals provides the steady hand that brings greater certainty and stability to transaction.

➤ Delivery
  • Delivery Exceptional service is our goal. We meet our clients’ unique needs with knowledgeable, caring and timely service. Our clients also can expect:
    1. professional, courteous conduct from everyone associated with the firm.
    2. coordination with other professionals to complement our services.

➤ Confidentiality
  • Confidentiality is paramount. We honor our clients’ privacy and protect their personal information. We:
    1. release confidential information to their other advisors only after proper authorization.
    2. maintain secure client records.
    3. use a client’s name as a reference only with prior approval.
